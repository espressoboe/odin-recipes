Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are absolutely delicious. You won't believe they ARE vegan!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup vanilla oatmilk
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • maple syrup, to serve

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, add the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir to combine.
  2. In a second bowl or large measuring cup, add oatmilk, apple cider vinegar and vanilla. Stir to combine.
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture and whisk until smooth.
  4. Let batter rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour about 1/2 cup of batter onto a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  6. When the top begins to bubble, flip the pancake and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup.
